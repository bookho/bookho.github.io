    Leaving the third dusk of Bran Village, Richard arrived The castle of Dan Baron, and saw the baron appointed by the Duke of the Wolf as a supply for himself.

    The Baron’s castle is built on a small island in the Great Lakes of Windermere, connected to the shore by an empty boulevard.

    This island is not naturally formed. It is the generation of the Baron family that has been slowly filled out from the lake. The cost of human and financial resources is countless and has been built over the past 100 years. It’s the size of the present.

    The outermost circle is a typical castle building with a sturdy circular tower and the roof is an arrowhead and a lookout. The buildings in the inner layer can be completely described by artworks. The vaults of various shapes, through the columns of two or three floors, and the terraces extending in all directions, are all gorgeous and exquisite.

    When you look out from the main building, you can also enjoy the lake and the water, as well as the mountains and forests. The castle has an old name, which means the dusk on the lake. Over time, it is simply called the dusk castle.

     When I first saw the castle, Richard’s instinctive reaction was how to attack. With his unprofessional vision, you can find three or four effective attacks, showing the weakness of the castle defense.

    The most powerful defense is either the road that is 20 meters long, crosses the lake from land, and leads to the castle gate. Although this road can accommodate eight heavy-loaded knights side by side, it still limits the number and speed of attackers. Next, Richard discovered that this is a very beautiful and charming castle. From the beginning of construction, it was not used for war.

    Before the visit, Richard heard that Baron Fontaine is a superior swordsman, and the baron who inherits the home swordsmanship currently has 14 levels of combat power. A well-deserved master of swordsmanship. The baron is also a painter and poet, and his love for painting and poetry is far more than swordsmanship.

    Different from the other vassals of the Duke of the Wolf, the Baron Fontaine is a very gentle person, and rarely expands abroad. The standing army in the territory is roughly eight hundred. Around the people, there are a hundred elite knights. It seems that the Baron’s military strength is very weak, but those who look down on him have suffered a lot. Fang Dan is a knowledgeable and versatile person, and he is also resourceful in his leadership battles. He has defeated several family enemies and expanded his territory to more than 500 square kilometers. This is in the Faro plane, which has surpassed the average Viscount.

    The baron is fertile and rich in grain. At the same time, minerals such as coal, iron and copper are abundant. Although there are no rare rare minerals, iron and copper are the law. The most commonly used weaponry raw materials for the Luo plane. Baron Fontain has established a large number of military workshops to produce a series of standard weapons from swords to bodyplates. For this reason, the average level of the baron’s military equipment can be said to be the highest among the princes of the Duke of the Wolf. It is also one of the largest weapons and equipment production sites in the Sequoia Kingdom.

     Richard placed most of his soldiers in the barracks outside the castle, and then only took a dozen followers into the dusk castle to meet with Baron Fontaine.

    The baron is about forty years old, and he is a young and energetic man. After meeting, he was amazed at the youngness of Richard, and at the dinner, he was convinced by Richard's rich and profound magical knowledge. The baron has three sons and seven daughters. Several of them are very fond of magic, so the baron asks them to come out and let Richard know and evaluate their future magical talents.

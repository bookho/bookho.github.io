    Spring.

    This is a delightful season. After a long winter, people can finally start a new year without having to endure the cold, the food is easier to get, and the variety is also Gradually become rich. So when winter comes to spring, it is the most important festival of the year. Not only humans, dwarves, orcs, elves and even ogres put the most important celebrations in spring. Of course, the world is so complicated, there will always be exceptions. For example, the residents of the underground world, spring has little meaning to them. Exceptions can go to extremes, such as the snow demon, the most hated of this race is spring. In any case, for most humans, spring is always enjoyable.

    When the warm and humid airflow climbed over the coastal mountains and reached the village of Rutherland, the villagers knew that spring was here again.

    Rutherland is located at the foot of the Coastal Mountains, a small, inconspicuous spot in this vast stretch of mountains, and tens of thousands of humans. One of the settlements. It is part of the Baron Tucker and is also the territory of the Holy Alliance. However, even in terms of straight-line distance, the castle of Baron Tucker is nearly three hundred kilometers away. Only in the autumn harvest, the village people will see the baron’s tax official. Only at this time, the lord has a negligible sense of existence. The baron’s tax is very light, just to collect some local products that are unique to the mountains, and have little influence on the daily life of the villagers. However, the output of the village is limited. If the taxation influence is larger, it will involve survival problems.

    The life in the mountains is actually not too difficult, as long as one year of hard work is over.

    When spring arrives, the land outside the village needs to be ploughed, sowing the food that can be harvested in the summer. The hunters began to enter the forest. At this time, those who were hungry for a winter in the depths of the forest gradually woke up and began to forage. They become very dangerous and extremely aggressive. But some of the specialties of Warcraft, such as some glands that can be used as precious herbs or spices, can only sell the highest price if they are the best at this time. Every year in this season, hunters will have death and injury, but every year hunters will enter the mountains as usual. So in the village of Rutherland, the forest and the goddess of hunting get the most faith. It is important to know that in addition to the dragon of eternal life, the gods and beliefs on the continent of Noland are like stars above the head.

    Noland is a land of fertile land, a land of strength, and a land of strict hierarchy.

    Even a remote and peaceful village like Rutherland, it is deeply imprinted with the brand. The villagers are honest and sincere, but they also respect the strong and despise the weak. There are only a few dozen small villages, but they also have hidden levels.

It's easy to get, just go to the woods next to the village and you'll be fine.

Civilians and aristocratic children in the town, this time most of them are still learning and practicing under the wings of their parents.

    The life in Rutherland Village is calm and slow, time is passing unconsciously, and in the twinkling of an eye, it is a new spring.

    Small Richard has grown a dozen centimeters tall and looks like a child of eight-nine-year-old. According to the tradition of Rutherland Village, he should have learned to make and lay traps, to hunt rabbits and other grass-eating little Warcraft.

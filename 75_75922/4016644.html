     Triggering insurance is the core technology of the new shells. It is also the focus of the test.

    In order to avoid collisions, missed hands and caused a false explosion, he painstakingly set up a total of three insurance.

    The first is that the fuse is separated from the shell and stored and transported until it is installed on site - the fuse is shaped like a cone with a handle, almost the size of the fist, and the tail It is threaded and can be screwed into the slot of the top of the projectile during installation, which is quick and convenient. Since the shells are loaded with double-base chemical gunpowder, it is difficult to detonate without the detonator, which fundamentally guarantees the safety of the logistics work.

    Next is the inertia insurance in the fuse.

    It is similar to a door lock. Under normal conditions, it is held by a hard spring and cannot move. When the projectile is fired, the huge inertia causes the lock cylinder to sit back. Overcome the resistance of the spring to open the latch and release the second pass.

    The principle is very simple, but it is not easy to achieve, the first two days are basically spent on this - if the spring is too hard, the lock core can not sit back Sufficient distance, the spring is too soft and not safe enough. This can only be adjusted slowly by Anna according to the test situation. After eight rounds of test, a more reliable spring compression data is obtained.

    The last insurance is a centrifugal detonator.

    It is also the most technically interesting thing in the fuse. Simply put, the detonator is embedded in a coin-sized semi-circular iron plate, which is usually tilted. The center of the fuse is fixed by a spring. At this moment, the needle, the detonator, and the stimulating medicine are not in a straight line. Even if the shell falls from a high place, the striker does not touch the detonator and avoids the risk of fire. The iron plate is only movable after the second insurance card has been released from the lock.

    Because of the action of the barrel of the barrel, the shrapnel will spin at a very high speed, and under the action of the centrifugal force, the inclined detonator will gradually return to the positive direction. Like the rotating gyro, the center of gravity moves closer to the center axis. This process is completed approximately two or three hundred meters after the exit, so that even if the muzzle is blocked or the branches are covered, the warhead will not explode in advance.

    "In the past three days, we have received more than 60% of the settlement applications, and nearly half of them, that is, 725 people migrated. Newcomers who are less than a month old! Not only that, but he also created a lot of analytical formulas, "arrival - settlement interval" is one of them.

    For newcomers, wait and see is almost an instinct, plus the difference in work pay, not enough time for housing down payment The same is true, so the average value method is used. The shorter the interval, the more loyal the king and the more confident the new king.

    Boiler heating system is not a complicated thing, its essence is to boil hot water, then the heat is piped to the room, and then spread out by the radiator. The carrier of thermal energy can be water or water vapor. Roland uses the latter because the upper limit of steam temperature is higher and it is more convenient to circulate. As long as the boiler is kept burning, the steam pressure generated will automatically fill the small high temperature water droplets in every corner of the pipeline, even the pump is not needed.

     Of course, steam heating is not without its shortcomings. The pipeline is always in a high-pressure environment. If the interface is not strong, it will easily leak or even explode. It is the skin that is open, so the quality of the installation is higher. Anna's black wire welding can guarantee the strength of the interface, plus the coating of Soroya, this shortcoming can be reduced to a minimum.

    At the same time, Roland also installed an automatic pressure reducing valve on the boiler. The mechanical principle is the same as that of the centrifugal governor on the steam engine. When the pressure in the furnace is too high, The high-speed rotating flywheel pulls up the valve and decompresses the exhaust.

    The last insurance is located at the junction of the boiler and the two main heating pipes. He asked Anna to engrave several grooves around the copper pipe to reduce its strength, and Cans are similar. Even if the pressure valve does not open properly, the explosion will occur in the boiler room without harming the people in the room.

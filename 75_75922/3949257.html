    The third witch is a hummingbird, a short witch, just like her nickname. When asked about ability, it is much more cautious than other witches. She just became an adult this winter, and her symmetrical dimples and exquisite facial features match her soft voice.

     Her ability is to "lighten the object", after attaching the magic to the object, it can greatly reduce its weight, according to her, close to zero . Roland let the hummingbird demonstrate it in the field with his own teacup and found that the cup could float in the air—that is, its weight was about equal to the air. Of course, in the eyes of people in this era, the air has no weight.

    It seems that this is why the Co-Assistance Club can cross the Grey Fort Western World without replenishment. With the help of the little girl, the bags of wheat and dried fish can be easily carried on the road, and only a few people can carry a lot of materials, which can greatly reduce the logistical pressure.

    The hummingbird, although an adult, did not derive branching abilities. Its main ability must be touched by the target, and it is ineffective for the living body. The larger the object, the longer the conversion time and the more magical it consumes. Once the conversion is complete, it can last for several hours.

    This ability seems to be very suitable for the transportation industry, and it is also promising in the face of the upcoming war, but there are too many unknown factors that must be further tested to determine.

    Roland writes at the bottom of the parchment: practice with stones of different weights, control the magic output, and make the effective time and failure time accurately controllable .

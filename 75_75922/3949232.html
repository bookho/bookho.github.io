    Bianbian Town West, Chishui River.

    The snow made the carriage difficult, and the party took half a day to haul the first field gun to the outside of the town.

     "What is this time?" Carter felt a little accustomed to the new things that the prince was constantly making. "A magnified musket?"

    "Almost." Roland commanded the man to remove the hook and personally adjust the angle of the gun to level the gun body and the poles into the snow.

    The principle of artillery and musket is exactly the same, so it is very appropriate to say that it is an enlarged version of the musket. The gun fired a 12-pound solid iron ball. The shells and gunpowder were pre-loaded into the raft and the wires were ignited. Roland refers to the mature designs in history and refers to the carpenter's creation of a wooden gun mount. In order to improve the durability, the parts which are more rubbed with the ground and which are subjected to the force at the time of launch are covered with iron.

    In order to make the gun mount and the wheel, Roland spent almost the same amount of time as the drill, and the three carpenters were busy for a week before they finished, especially the diameter For the half-man high wheel, the carpenter first shaved four lengths of square wood strips. After soaking, they were fired to bend with fire, the ends were cut out and the grooves were spliced, and the outer edge of the wheel was covered with iron. This set of processes took four days.

    So in the eyes of Roland, this hand-crafted, limited-edition 12-pound field gun has a dazzling glow. When I pulled out the test, the lineup was very large.      "Do it according to the process, first clean up the cannon." Although Roland is very clear about the structure of the artillery, the specific operation is blank.

    The next day, the prince ushered in another good news.

    The factory for the production of steam engines was finally completed. The factory is located on the opposite bank of the Chishui River, close to the west end of the test field. The entire site has been fenced with wooden fences, and a simple road leading to the pontoon is also flattened. In Roland's plan, this place will be the industrial park after the town.

    Because of the need to expand the building space as much as possible, the factory is built from lighter weight wood and covers an area of ​​about 1,000 square meters. The machine tools made by Anna have been shipped in, respectively Two steam boring machines, two manual milling machines, a manual grinding machine and a manual lathe. Although these machines are simple in principle, the workmanship is first-class – except that the base or the pedal is made of wood, and the other parts are cut from wrought iron or steel. It can be said that at the level of existing materials, The highest precision machining is achieved.

    The workers in the factory are only a pitiful ten-placer. They were originally blacksmiths and apprentices in the town’s blacksmith shop. They are now being used by Roland for fifty silver wolves. High salary is recruited here to learn how to operate high-end machine tools. Their iron tools and blacksmith shops have also moved to a brick house outside the factory. In addition to creating some unimportant parts, the steam engine will be produced entirely by machine tools.

    Roland held a ribbon-cutting ceremony at the door of the factory. In order to strengthen his momentum, he recruited officials from the city hall and delivered a short speech. In the sound of the echo of the echo simulation, the Grey Fort Industrial Company was officially announced.

    In the next few days, Roland, who is the company’s executive, ran to the factory when he was free, demonstrating the operation of the machine tool and the manufacture of the steam engine. Process. To let an illiterate blacksmith master how to process and assemble a steam engine, the best way he can think of is to solve the flow chart. Similar to the assembly instructions of the model, the part names, numbers, and dimensions are marked one by one, and then they are installed. The order and connection are depicted in the form of a graph. At this point, Soroya helped him a lot.

    In the first few days of production, as he expected, almost none of the parts met the requirements, let alone spell out a complete steam engine. However, Roland does not care about the scrap rate. In the hands of Anna, these defective products can be recast into new plates in the blink of an eye. He believes that after repeated drills, it is sooner or later that these blacksmiths master the production methods of the new era.

    The faceless person has quietly crossed the canal and swept past the mercenary camp before the sun completely settled.

     Her real name is not called a faceless person, but Afra – this is the name given to her by Heather, the bishop of the holy city of Hermes. It means dust. She likes the name very much, because the dust is simple and unpretentious. As long as it falls on the ground, she can no longer distinguish each other, just like her.

     Only in front of Heather, Afra will restore his original appearance.

    As a member of the arbitral tribunal, she assisted the bishop in dealing with many fallen people, including witches who rebelled from the church and those who were corrupted by the world. Being sent to the kingdom is to complete a key task: to transform a pious presiding judge into the King of the Grey Fort. As for the capture of the fallen witch, it is just a leisurely activity. She likes to imitate the witches who have been tortured and tortured. Then she will experience it from beginning to end and share the pain of the fallen person so that she can feel more deeply what she has done. The meaning of the matter is also the atonement for her possession of the power of the devil.

    The camp of the other party is very cleverly placed, just in the field of the bulging shore, it is difficult to observe their whereabouts in the low places, and the witch flying in the sky Let her not dare to get too close. Afra has to lurk in a farm warehouse and wait until dark.

    When the night shrouded the earth, she unexpectedly noticed a change.

    The mercenaries have withdrawn from the dock area and have all been withdrawn from the camp, and the idiots of the dream waters are sparsely pulling up the torches and gathering together on the banks of the river - - This kind of battle is like telling the other party that "someone is coming to attack the camp."     There are still people around the camp patrolling, the campfire is very strong, the figures come and go, seemingly orderly, and there is no mess.

    They didn’t even choose to retreat?

Although I don't know what the other party is considering, I decided to stay here instead of escaping as soon as possible, but the outcome is already doomed. At this time, it does not need to be so laborious. In the blink of an eye, she turns herself into a mercenary. Although the effect can only last for half a day, it is enough for the assassination.

But in the face of the mercenary's weapons, she felt a little confused - it was an iron gun with a wooden handle, but the head did not have a gun tip, but a black hole.

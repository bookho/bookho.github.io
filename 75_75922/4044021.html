    Under the rush of molten iron, silicon and manganese are first oxidized, and then the carbon in the pig iron is turned.

    In the high temperature of nearly 1,500 degrees, carbon combines with oxygen in the air to form carbon monoxide, and the heat released causes the furnace to "boiling". The flame from the mouth of the furnace almost engulfs the blowpipe and the hob – this is why the bracket also covers the heat-resistant coating.


    A large amount of carbon monoxide escapes outwards, and is in contact with the outside air at the mouth of the furnace, followed by intense combustion, which is like a furnace. The violent sound of the gas expansion overshadowed the sound of the steam engine for a time. Everyone could not help but retire before the scene like a volcanic squirt. Only Roland stood with his hands and stood up against the hot wind, completely immersed in the roar of the steel flame. in.

    Unfortunately, the current technology can only be filled with air to make steel, and if it can be replaced with pure oxygen in the future, the fireworks will be even brighter.

    The last reaction is phosphorus and sulfur. When the oxides of the two and the limestone form slag, the flame begins to retreat. At this time, the molten iron is basically steel. . The lighter slag floats on the molten steel. As long as the converter is tilted, the molten steel can be poured out of the tapping port like pouring tea.

    If in accordance with the rules of modern steel mills, it is necessary to use the slag ball, the slag dart, and the infrared detection method to avoid the upper scum flowing into the steel ladle. in. No winter city's current technology can't do this, so Roland's solution is also very simple, that is, don't fall too clean. When there is still a small amount of molten steel left in the furnace, the furnace is directly lifted, and then they are poured together with the slag. If you waste a little, you will waste a little. If you collect more, you can return to the furnace to refine it. Anyway, he does not have to pursue any production benefits.

    In order to ensure the quality of steel, the finishing work is to remove excess oxygen from the molten steel.

    The bauxite that is continuously transported from the long song fortress can act as a deoxidizer - a bucket of pure aluminum separated from Lucia is poured In the molten steel, the generated alumina slag is floated together with the unreacted aluminum to the surface layer, and the entire steelmaking process is completed.

     In theology, monotheism believes that there is only one god.     The Tao Te Ching was regarded by the Taipings as the creation of the Taoist god "Tao" word by word to his spokesperson Laozi (Li Er).     Teaching

    Unique and Creation: Taiping believes that "Changed three times, thus creating everything in the world." In the Taiping religion, the number three is sacred.

    Trinity: Most Taiping believers believe that the only ones are Yuqing, Shangqing and Taiqing, namely Sanqing. The master of Yuqing is Yuanshi Tianzun, the master of the Qing Dynasty is Lingbao Tianzun, and the Lord of Taiqing is the moral Tianzun (Tai Shang Laojun, Laozi).

    Redemption and Doomsday: Taiping believes that the present world (Cangtian) will eventually end and replace it with the next life (Yellow). In a certain year (a specific time or a symbolic time is controversial), all the people in the world will be judged according to their faith in the Tao and the execution of Germany.

    The Taiping Catholic Theology believes that since Laozi (Li Er) preached the message of the right way, all the sages, sages and celestial beings are propping the same message. . The Taiping scriptures refer to traditional Taoists and celestialists as “the sects” to distinguish these religions from Confucianism, Buddhism, and Western polytheism. They think that Li Er is the embodiment of Dao Shen, and Zhang Ling, who established the church, is the first Tianshi. The mainstream and the Hengshui faction believe that only the surname Zhang can be the master of heaven and the teacher, and the Oriental Tianshi and the New World do not agree with this view.

    In the 11th century, Zhang Zai (Zhang Hengqu) introduced science to reform, which is also the first religious reform in history, than the 15th century religion of the Orthodox Church The reform was more than four hundred years old.

    The symbol of Taiping religion is the Taiji diagram. The mainstream school advocates the yellow headscarf that represents the final judgment, so it is often called the yellow towel.

    History

    At the beginning, the Taiping religion was a repressed and persecuted religion in the Eastern Empire. As a result, a large-scale yellow towel uprising broke out. In the third generation of the Zhang Lu period, the Yellow Turban Uprising, which was enshrined in the Eastern Han Empire, merged. In the name of "the world is peaceful", a unified Taiping Church was established, with Zhang Jiao as the ruling party and Zhang Lu as the teacher. This righteousness essentially destroyed the Han Empire and continued to plague the successor regimes of the Wei and Jin who inherited its political power. It was not until the split of the East and West churches during the Qian Qian period that this problem was initially resolved.


     Before the end of the second century of the standard calendar, the Taiping religion spread the Eastern Empire and reached today’s West Tang, Murray, Jiaonan, Weizhou and other places. The farthest reaches the territory of the Elven Empire. At the height of the elf empire, the introduction of monotheistic theory had a great influence on the Church of the Sun God. The theory of the "right way" and "church" also greatly influenced the development of the underground churches at that time. Evolution. It is generally believed that the "covenant" thinking of the orthodox and the Dagong Church and the perfect bishop/bishop system come from the organizational structure of the "right way" and "canal" and "square" of the Taiping Taoist church.

    The establishment of the Taiping Church is a religious system of political and religious unity. In the 10 years of Zhangjiao’s military training, this “Peaceful State” already has nearly one-half of the Eastern Empire, and the entire Western Region and part of the South. But the fact that this polity has two sects innately led to the failure of the uprising. Zhang Lu was defeated by Liu Bei, the famous empire of the Eastern Empire, and led the Hanzhong Army to surrender Cao Cao; Zhang Jiao, who led the main army to return, died in the west of the Guanguan Pass, and the Taiping Taoist forces withdrew from the Eastern Empire, and their forces shifted to Murray in the future. Manchu.


     After Zhang Liang’s death, many civil wars broke out in the Taiping Taoist countries until Sun En and Lu Xun established a stable Taiping Taoist system. During this period, although the Taiping Civil War continued, its beliefs and power maps continued to spread to the surrounding areas, and brought a general order to the chaotic and long-established Murrayman Islands and the peninsula, and established a brilliant Taiping. Tao civilization.

    The disputes between the churches of the East Zhang Lu-Ge Xuan-Xu Xun were intermittent, and finally the differences reached the peak when Qian Qianzhi mastered the Eastern Tianshi School. Qi Qianzhi reached a consensus with the imperial court at that time, ending the Taiping Tao uprising that lasted for more than two hundred years, transforming the Eastern Church into a religious organization under the imperial power, but it also led to the complete division of the Eastern and Western Taiping. Since then, the Eastern Taiping Church has been generally called "Tianshi", which is different from the mainstream.

    Standard calendar 7th century, the Tang Empire was established, its royal family is Li, and the same ancestor of the Taiping religion (Li Er), the Taiping religion became the Tang Empire , legalized with the Chang'an Order. The Taoists regard this political achievement as a victory of the Tao. Taiping teaches into the golden age. From the Ridge Mountains and the east of the Qianlong Sea, all of them are under the radiation of Taiping.

     During this period, the Taoist people continued to expand through the use of force and peaceful missions. The trade route of Taipingism also extended to Interlake, Pazhou, and Spice Islands. And established a second mission center in the later Western Tang Dynasty. The golden age created new developments in law, philosophy, and religion. The main Daoist collections were compiled and compiled in the Tang Xuanzong period (the “Kaiyuan Daozang”), and most of them continued to the modern main monastic gates, such as Maoshan, Shenque, Kunlun, and Lushan, which were also founded in this golden age. At this time, the Elf Empire and the first dynasty of the Holy Coman Empire have collapsed. The Western world has fallen into a dark age. A large number of priests, scholars, and magicians have taken refuge in the Taiping World. Cultural exchanges have brought unprecedented prosperity. Qingniu, Xiping Both the government and Changan have become world-class metropolises.

    But the good times are not long, the standard calendar began in the ninth century, with the civil war and de facto division of the Tang Empire, the warlords everywhere, the Eastern Empire was caught in war and recession, The barbarians on the border and the barbarian army being recruited will be rampant in the empire, and the barbaric regimes at the border will be established one by one, and the internal ambitions will come one after another. The education and legitimacy of the Taiping religion was seriously challenged by the decline of the Eastern Empire. During this period, the Taiping religion repeatedly organized the guardian army to protect the Holy Land and paid a great price. This has caused the pace of Taiping's expansion to the west to stop.


     Since the rebuilding of the Holy Empire in the eleventh century, the emperor of the empire launched a series of wars of the Southern Expedition to counter the occupation of the southern territory of the Empire by the Taiping Road. The initial Nanzheng was very successful. The Empire established a series of Principality and Houguo on the East Intres, the Xiankti Peninsula, Qianlong Island, and even the Hu Island and the Murrayman Peninsula. It was possible for the original empire to completely collect the southern islands as territories, but their achievements were once again conquered by the later Taiping religious reform leader Zhang Zai and his successors. Zhang Zai’s disciple Han Gang moved the capital of the Taiping Taoist country to the Taiping Mansion on Qianlong Island as a center and confronted the Holy Empire.

     In the 12th century, during the reign of Fang La, the countries of the Taiping Road once turned their attention back to the East. But in this historical period known as the "black tide", the entire Tang Empire and all its internal vassal states, as well as the nearby civilized barbarian countries, were almost completely swept away by the unprecedented powerful black invaders, the undead iron hoof. Stepping through the Green Bull House, only Taiping and Xiping House survived. Lu Yun came to power to form a great alliance against the plague of the undead, and eventually defeated them.

    The civil war of the Holy Coman Empire brought a new type of religion called "Protestantism" and a new regime called the "Republic of the Union". This regime broke out with a powerful fighting power that was incomparable by the decaying sacred empire. It not only seized the East Intres, but also opened up the entire sea route through the East and West in 50 years, and its tentacles reached the mainland of the Eastern Empire. A series of failures have caused Taiping to lose control of Muleiman and the Western Tang Dynasty.     Sects

     Like all major religions, the Taiping religion is also full of sects, these sects The nature of religious beliefs is the same, but there are obvious differences in theology and law. The Tianshi School and the Yellow Towel School are the most basic divisions. The Hengshui School is considered a mysticism of Taipingism and represents the combination of Confucianism and Taipingism.

    Original Taoism: The original Taoism was the original Taoism before Zhang Ling and Zhang Lu reform.

    Yellow towel school: The yellow towel school is led by the great Tianshi Zhang Jiao, after the Western Expedition arrived in Murrayman to establish the mainstream faction.

    Tianshi School: The Tianshi School is a faction established by Zhang Lu and his successors who remained in the Eastern Empire. The forces are mainly in the Eastern Empire.

    The Hengshou School: The Hengshui School was established by Zhang Zai, and Zhang Sanfeng’s perfect post-reformation faction advocated Qi Xue and the integration of the three religions.

    Basic faction: The basics believe that only moral ethics is the sole foundation of Taiping Road, and does not recognize the works of Tianshi later.

    The whole truth (heresy): The forces of the whole truth are mainly in the New World. They think that Laozi’s westbound reaches the New World, and the New World’s monks can also become heavens. division.

The other is to go east along the Blackwater River to Macabu, where it returns to the south through the waterway. Both roads are full of unknown dangers and will be long and harsh. Siegel was awkward, and no one was there to guide him. It was useless to pray to God, and he must make this decision as soon as possible.

The orc patrols were encountered on the road, but Siegel found them far away and hid them in time. The orcs all went in the same direction, the town of bacon. If you go later, you are likely to be trapped in the town and not get out.

This will cause the Master's feet to sink into the snow, and the speed of action will be greatly reduced, but it will save physical strength.

    The emergence of the Black River made Siegel a sigh of relief. However, Siegel does not believe that the body can distinguish whether the flame is a friend or an enemy? Under the water that never freezes, there are also dense trees growing here, enough to cover those dangerous sights. Siegel found a comfortable tree hole that night, and he put thick pine needles inside to squeeze in to avoid the cold caused by the cold outside. Siegel has not ignited for a long time to cook food, and now has a chance. He built a simple wall outside the tree hole with snow, which could both block the fire and protect the wind. The key space allows the food inside to not spoil, but it does not preserve the temperature of the food. Siegel took out carrots, onions and turnips, a small packet of salt. If there are other materials, such as potatoes or tomatoes, it will make him happier, but not too much in the wild, which is enough for him to spend a comfortable night.

It didn't take long for Siegel to eat stewed rabbit meat, and Barron was enjoying the minced meat that Siegel had helped him with.

    The story of this book takes place in an era of change in the world, the conflict between old and new forces, and more things that have not yet been revealed in the book, destined to make this world It is a world of disorder and chaos. Therefore, for him, the stability of the empire and the sustainable development of the future are his everything.

    The capitalists are completely on the opposite side of Magus, in the past they were small merchants of aristocratic fish, but in the new era created by Magus, money has already From a certain symbol to a weapon, a weapon that can pierce all hearts. Money can buy everything they need, whether it is the dignity, status, decent and power they have not had in the past, or the enjoyment and satisfaction they have.

    This is destined to allow them to focus on their own pursuit of their own career, fighting each other and ignoring something that is of no importance to them.

He is more concerned with the officials who have corrupted the capitalists and how many people have become representatives of their interests.
